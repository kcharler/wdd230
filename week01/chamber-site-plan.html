Abidjan Business Chamber Site Plan

Client: Abobo Municipality

Service: Web Development: Chamber of Commerce website

Lead Developer: Romeo Koui

Overview

Abidjan is a beautiful and vibrant community, full of welcoming and industrious people. The area is booming in development, and it members wants to add more value in it community through infastructure, commerce, technology, agriculture,education, culture and art. Sharing the Abidjan experience nation wide. The Abidjan Chamber of Commerce as a bridge that connects the outside to Abidjan and Abidjan to the outside world.

Purpose

The Abidjan Chamber of Commerce website serves as a dynamic and comprehensive platform aimed at fostering economic growth and development within the region. Dedicated to promoting trade, investment, and business excellence, the website provides a central hub for local and international businesses to access valuable resources, stay informed about industry trends, and connect with potential partners. Through a user-friendly interface, the website facilitates the exchange of information, trade opportunities, and regulatory updates, empowering businesses in Abidjan to thrive and contribute to the overall prosperity of the community. Whether assisting entrepreneurs in navigating regulatory landscapes or promoting networking events, the Abidjan Chamber of Commerce website plays a pivotal role in creating a vibrant and collaborative business ecosystem.

Intended Solutions:

  • Home Page:The hompage serves as the primary gateway to the website. It provides a welcoming introduction to the organizatiion, conveying its mission and values. Clear navigation allows for easy access to other sections of the website. It offers latest news and updates, weather information, etc.
  • Discover Page:is the hub for showcasing the community. It purpose is to highlight attractions, landmarks, history, local businesses, culture and demographic.
  • Directory Page: serves a comprehensive resource for finding and connecting with the chamber member businesses. It aims to promote the businesses of members, foster networking and facilitate partnership btoh in the community and nation wide.
  • Join Page:is dedicated to encouraging new memberships and informing potential members about the benefits of joining Abidjan Chamber of Commerce. There are three membership level options of non-profit, silver, and gold. The different levels have different perks including directory listings, publications, trainings, social media work, luncheons, sponsorships, advertising, etc.

Scenarios

  • Business Owner - Sidonie:Sidonie is a local business owner who moved to the area quite recently and is interested in networking opportunities. Sidonie's question: "What events will the chamber be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking?"
  • Community Advocate - Romeo:Romeo is a community advocate and is curious about the chamber's leadership and decision-making process. Romeo's question: "Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors?"
  • Prospective Resident - Marie-France:Marie-France is considering moving to the area and wants to know more about the community's growth and development. Marie-France's question: "What has been the population growth in the area?"
