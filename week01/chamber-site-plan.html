Abobo Business Chamber Site Plan

Client: Abobo Municipality

Service: Web Development: Chamber of Commerce website

Lead Developer: Romeo Koui

Overview

Abobo is a beautiful and vibrant community, full of welcoming and industrious people. The area is booming in development, and it members wants to add more value in it community through infastructure, commerce, technology, agriculture,education, culture and art. Sharing the Abobo experience nation wide. The Abobo Chamber of Commerce as a bridge that connects the outside to Abobo and Abobo to the outside world.

Purpose

Abobo Business Chamber website would serve as a one stop online resource for all local businesses and community members within the Abobo area. Our mission is to foster economic development, prioritizing the growth of the local businessman and woman, and pushing for lucrative business policies and ideas in our community. As a chamber of commerce organization, we provide a wide range of services which includes, networking events, fundraising in support of small businesses, creating an environment for educational opportunities and many more. By bringing local businesses together, connecting with each other, and the community being at the heart of it, we can achieve a thriving local economy. Together as one family unit, we can build a stronger and more prosperous community for all.

Intended Solutions:

  • Home Page:The hompage serves as the primary gateway to the website. It provides a welcoming introduction to the organizatiion, conveying its mission and values. Clear navigation allows for easy access to other sections of the website. It offers latest news and updates, weather information, etc.
  • Discover Page:is the hub for showcasing the community. It purpose is to highlight attractions, landmarks, history, local businesses, culture and demographic.
  • Directory Page: serves a comprehensive resource for finding and connecting with the chamber member businesses. It aims to promote the businesses of members, foster networking and facilitate partnership btoh in the community and nation wide.
  • Join Page:is dedicated to encouraging new memberships and informing potential members about the benefits of joining Abobo Chamber of Commerce. There are three membership level options of non-profit, silver, and gold. The different levels have different perks including directory listings, publications, trainings, social media work, luncheons, sponsorships, advertising, etc.

Scenarios

  • Business Owner - Sidonie:Sidonie is a local business owner who moved to the area quite recently and is interested in networking opportunities. Sidonie's question: "What events will the chamber be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking?"
  • Community Advocate - Romeo:Romeo is a community advocate and is curious about the chamber's leadership and decision-making process. Romeo's question: "Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors?"
  • Prospective Resident - Marie-France:Marie-France is considering moving to the area and wants to know more about the community's growth and development. Marie-France's question: "What has been the population growth in the area?"
